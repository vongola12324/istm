Faculty Search

The Media Lab is searching for a new tenure-
track junior faculty member. Candidates should have a strong record of research and practice, a willingness to take risks, a desire to look beyond traditional disciplines, and a dedication to making a difference in the world. A doctorate is preferred but not required.

Application deadline: July 15, 2016.

Featured Project
Quantizer

Quantizer

The Responsive Environments group, in collaboration with the ATLAS Outreach Team, has built Quantizer, a platform in which real-time experimental particle physics data becomes a canvas for musical expression.

Visit the Quantizer website
 
to hear a set of musical compositions driven by real-time particle collisions in the ATLAS detector and to inquire about creating your own data-driven sound.
